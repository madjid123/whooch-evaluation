Economic integration has become one of the features of the modern era, and the joining of economic blocs has greatly accelerated due to the advantages that the latter bears from the expansion of the market size, expansion in the volume of production, creating a suitable climate for economic development and advancing the wheel of economic growth, given that the establishment of the bloc generates an appropriate environment to benefit from development technology and contribute to building a solid economic base. This is what led the countries with homogeneous ingredients to unite in the form of economic blocs, and one of the most prominent points on which an economic bloc focuses is the activation of foreign direct investment because it is the main engine of economic growth and the existence of a strong correlation between investment rates, growth rates, economic development and the degree of economic growth In addition, foreign investments are a source of financing development projects and obtaining the technology needed to achieve economic progress and growth.
